About Me

I used to see my cousin using a computer, playing games, using social media sites and wonder how everything is going on. How is facebook.com running on his phone? How is the GTA Vice City Police doesn't do anything even on a 5 star when a cheatcode "LeaveMeAlone" is entered. This curiosity of technology made me study Computer Science in Ramapo College of New Jersey. Now I'm into web development and data analysis. I'm learning and I still have many questions in my head about Computer Science and Technology. These question are motivating me to move forward, to learn everyday, to keep up with this evergrowing technological advancement.
